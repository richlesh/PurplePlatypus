Here's a comparison table for the three iPads across Apple's current lineup:

# iPad A16 vs. iPad Air M4 vs. iPad Pro M5

| Spec | **iPad (A16)** | **iPad Air (M4)** | **iPad Pro (M5)** |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Tier** | Entry-level | Mid-range | Flagship |
| **Released** | March 2025 | 2025 | October 22, 2025 |
| **Chip** | A16 Bionic (6-core CPU, 5-core GPU) | Apple M4 (9-core CPU, 9/10-core GPU) | Apple M5 (9–10-core CPU, 10-core GPU w/ Neural Accelerator per core) |
| **Display** | 11" Liquid Retina LCD | 11" or 13" Liquid Retina LCD | 11" or 13" Ultra Retina XDR (Tandem OLED) |
| **Refresh rate** | 60Hz | 60Hz | ProMotion, adaptive 10–120Hz |
| **RAM** | 6GB | 8GB | 12GB (16GB on 1TB/2TB models) |
| **Storage** | 128GB / 256GB / 512GB | 128GB / 256GB / 512GB / 1TB | 256GB / 512GB / 1TB / 2TB |
| **Biometrics** | Touch ID (top button) | Touch ID (top button) | Face ID |
| **Front camera** | 12MP Center Stage (landscape) | 12MP Center Stage (landscape) | 12MP Center Stage (landscape) |
| **Rear camera** | 12MP wide | 12MP wide | 12MP wide + LiDAR Scanner |
| **Connectivity** | Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth 5.3 | Wi-Fi 7 (Apple N1), BT, C1X cellular | Wi-Fi 7 (Apple N1), BT, C1X cellular |
| **Ports** | USB-C | USB-C | USB-C (Thunderbolt / USB 4) |
| **Apple Pencil** | Pencil Pro & USB-C | Pencil Pro & USB-C | Pencil Pro & USB-C |
| **Starting price** | ~$349 (US)* | ~$599 | ~$999 (11") / ~$1,299 (13") |

\* *Note: Some regions/retailers reported a price adjustment on the iPad A16 (up to ~$449); Apple's baseline launch price was $349.*

### Quick takeaways
- **iPad A16** — Best value for everyday tasks (browsing, streaming, notes). LCD, 60Hz, Touch ID.
- **iPad Air M4** — The "sweet spot": desktop-class M4 chip and a size choice (11"/13"), but keeps the LCD/60Hz display.
- **iPad Pro M5** — The premium pick: stunning Tandem OLED display, ProMotion 120Hz, Face ID, LiDAR, Thunderbolt, and the fastest chip with big AI/GPU gains.
